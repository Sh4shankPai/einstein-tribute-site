Albert Einstein

What is Einstein's greatest achievement?

Albert Einstein's greatest achievement was the development of the theory of relativity, which includes the special theory of relativity (1905) and the general theory of relativity (1915). These theories revolutionized our understanding of space, time, and gravity, leading to profound advancements in physics and technology.

What major awards did he win in his lifetime?

Major Awards Won:

  • Nobel Prize in Physics (1921)
  • Copley Medal (1925)
  • Matteucci Medal (1923)
  • Max Planck Medal (1929)
